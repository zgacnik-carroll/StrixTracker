About StrixTracker

StrixTracker is your personal companion for logging and tracking owl sightings. Keep your observations organized, discover patterns over time, and contribute to a growing community of wildlife enthusiasts.

Tips for Spotting Owls

  • Observe quietly at dusk or dawn when owls are most active.
  • Bring a flashlight or use red-light filters to avoid disturbing wildlife.
  • Note the location, species, and behavior of each sighting for accurate logging.
